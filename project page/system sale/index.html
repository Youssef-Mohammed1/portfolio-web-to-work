An advanced POS and inventory management system crafted for seamless sales tracking, smart stock control, and a modern user experience across all devices.

The Narrative

The system features a modern and well-structured dashboard designed to simplify store management operations through a clean and intuitive user interface. It provides a complete point-of-sale experience where users can manage products, create invoices, monitor stock quantities, and track sales activity efficiently. The platform is built around a fully connected workflow where all sections of the system interact with each other seamlessly. Products added in the inventory page instantly appear in the sales page without requiring any manual updates, creating a smooth and organized management experience.

The sales process is directly connected to the reports and analytics section, where invoices and transaction records are stored automatically after every sale. Product quantities are deducted from the inventory in real time once a sale is completed, ensuring accurate stock tracking and reducing management errors. If a product is removed from an invoice or returned, its quantity is automatically restored to the inventory. The system also provides detailed sales statistics, invoice history, daily revenue calculations, and top-selling product analysis through a modern and responsive dashboard. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and LocalStorage, the platform delivers a fast, reliable, and fully responsive experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

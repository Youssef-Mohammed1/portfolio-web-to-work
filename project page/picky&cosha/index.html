The Narrative

Beki & Kosha is a modern crochet storefront designed to deliver a warm and elegant handmade shopping experience through a clean and visually refined user interface. The website focuses on presenting handcrafted crochet products in an organized and attractive way, allowing customers to browse collections smoothly while enjoying a soft feminine aesthetic that reflects the brand identity. The platform combines elegant layouts, balanced spacing, and carefully selected colors to create a cozy and engaging online shopping atmosphere.

The store features responsive product sections, category-based organization, smooth navigation, and visually appealing product cards that help improve customer interaction and increase sales. Each section of the website is structured to guide users naturally through the browsing experience, from discovering products to exploring brand information and contacting the store بسهولة. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the project delivers a fast, modern, and fully responsive experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices while maintaining strong visual consistency and a premium handmade brand presentation.
